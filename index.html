Who I Am

I'm a passionate IT engineering student specializing in Intelligent Communicating & Mobile Systems at FST Fez, Morocco. My world revolves around networks, security protocols, and building systems that are both resilient and hard to break into.

What drives me isn't just the technical challenge — it's knowing that the firewalls I configure, the vulnerabilities I scan for, and the encrypted streams I architect are what keep data and people safe. I got my first real taste of this during my internship at SNRT Morocco, where I deployed production-grade firewall infrastructure and helped broadcast AFCON 2025 in live HD with zero packet loss.

I'm actively seeking an internship in network security where I can bring this energy into a professional team.
